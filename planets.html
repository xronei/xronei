BIODIVERSITY

Earth is a planet of incredible diversity, hosting a wide range of ecosystems, climates, and life forms. From towering mountains and vast oceans to lush forests and arid deserts, each region contributes to the planet’s uniqueness.
Biodiversity, the variety of life on Earth, includes millions of species of plants, animals, and microorganisms.

This intricate web of life supports essential processes like oxygen production, pollination, and nutrient cycling. However, biodiversity is under threat due to deforestation, pollution, and climate change. Protecting Earth's diversity is crucial for maintaining ecological balance and ensuring a sustainable future for all life forms.
